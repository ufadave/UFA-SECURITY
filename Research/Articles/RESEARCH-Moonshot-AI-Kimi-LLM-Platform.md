# RESEARCH — Moonshot AI (Kimi)

## Source
- **URL:** https://www.moonshot.ai/
- **Date:** 2026-05-03

## What It Is
Moonshot AI is a Chinese AI company (founded 2023) developing the Kimi family of large language models. Their flagship model is **Kimi K2.6** — a natively multimodal, open-weight mixture-of-experts model with 1 trillion total parameters (32B active), 256K context window, and leading benchmarks for agentic coding tasks. The company is pursuing AGI and has an API platform at `platform.moonshot.ai`. The latest release (K2.6, April 2026) is currently the top-ranked open-weight model on the Vals Index.
