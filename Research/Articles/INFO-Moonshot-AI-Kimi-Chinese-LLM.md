# INFO-Moonshot-AI-Kimi-Chinese-LLM

**Source:** https://www.moonshot.ai/
**Date:** 2026-05-05

---

## What It Is

Moonshot AI is a Beijing-based AI company (founded 2023, Tsinghua alumni) that develops large language models under the "Kimi" brand. Their flagship model Kimi K2.5 (released January 2026) is a natively multimodal 1-trillion-parameter MoE LLM, open-sourced under a modified MIT license, with strong performance in coding and agentic tasks. Anthropic has accused Moonshot of using fraudulent accounts to scrape Claude conversations for training data (February 2026).

## Relevance

Low — file for reference. Not a direct security tool or operational concern. Relevant context if evaluating Chinese AI models for any internal use — data residency and sovereignty concerns apply given Beijing HQ and Alibaba/Tencent investor involvement. The training data controversy with Anthropic is worth noting if Kimi or the API is ever proposed internally.
