## What It Is

TrustedSec blog post discussing how GRC programs need to adapt to the pace of AI tooling adoption — balancing the risk of moving too slowly (falling behind threat actors and productivity gains) against moving too fast (inadequate controls, compliance gaps, data exposure). Likely covers policy frameworks for AI tool governance, risk acceptance processes, and how to avoid GRC becoming a blocker rather than an enabler.
