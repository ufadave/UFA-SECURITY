# INFO -- Profero GenAI Readiness Assessment (Rapid-IR)

**Source:** https://profero.io/genai-assessment/
**Date:** 2026-06-16
**Author:** Profero (Rapid-IR platform)

---

## What It Is

Profero's GenAI Readiness Assessment is a two-day, in-environment security review
focused on AI incident readiness -- built by the same IR team that responds to AI
incidents, not a separate audit practice. Delivered as part of Profero's Rapid-IR
platform (which includes Readiness, Response, Discovery, and Intelligence quadrants).

Unlike questionnaire-based AI risk assessments, Profero's offering focuses specifically
on the ability to *investigate* AI incidents, not just prevent them. Assessment findings
are maintained in Rapid-IR's Readiness quadrant and feed into a continuous readiness
score updated against active Profero IRT casework. The platform provides a 20-minute
guaranteed IR response when the AI incident actually hits.

**Profero IRT context:** The team has documented "AI-Induced Destruction" as a new attack
pattern, reverse engineered AI-integrated RATs (WindowsAudit.exe using Discord C2 with
EDR teardown), and monitors Iranian/Russian hacktivist activity. They're a credible IR-first
shop, not a compliance-oriented assessor.

## Relevance

Low-Medium -- filed as a vendor reference for future consideration. The AI Acceptable Use
Policy was just delivered for director sign-off, and the GreyNoise POC is underway. A formal
GenAI readiness assessment is an appropriate next-horizon consideration as AI tool adoption
increases (Codex, ChatGPT, future Copilot Enterprise deployment).

The framing of "assess your ability to investigate AI incidents, not just prevent them" is
directly aligned with the current gap: detection and response workflows for AI tool abuse
(prompt injection, token theft via Copilot) are not yet formally assessed.
